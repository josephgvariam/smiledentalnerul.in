As firm believers in good dental care and well thought out clinical processes, Smile Dental Clinic includes support for dental treatments such as restorative (dental restorations, crowns, bridges), orthodontics (braces), prosthodontic (dentures, crown/bridge), endodontic (root canal) therapy, periodontal (gum) therapy, and oral surgery (extraction of teeth), as well as performing examinations, taking radiographs (x-rays) and diagnosis. Additionally, our dentists can further engage in oral surgery procedures such as dental implant placement.

Specialities

We offer a variety of dental treatments that are carried out to prevent or treat the two most common oral diseases which are dental caries (tooth decay) and periodontal disease (gum disease or pyorrhea). Common treatments involve the restoration of teeth, extraction or surgical removal of teeth, scaling and root planing and endodontic root canal treatment.

  • Endodontics - Root canal therapy,diseases of the dental pulp.
  • Oral and maxillofacial pathology - diagnosis and the treatment of oral and maxillofacial related diseases.
  • Oral and maxillofacial radiology - radiologic interpretation of oral and maxillofacial diseases.
  • Oral and maxillofacial surgery - Extractions, implants, and MaxilloFacial surgery which also includes correction of congenital facial deformities.
  • Orthodontics and dentofacial orthopaedics - The straightening of teeth and modification of midface and mandibular growth.
  • Periodontology (periodontics) - treatment of diseases of the gums (non-surgical and surgical) as well as placement and maintenance of dental implants.
  • Pediatric dentistry (pedodontics) - Dentistry for children
  • Prosthodontics - Dentures, bridges and the restoration of implants.
